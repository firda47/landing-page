Frequently Asked Questions

Do you cater to vegans/vegetarians?

Yes! We have specialized plans for Vegan, Vegetarian, Keto, Paleo, and Gluten-Free diets. You can select your preference during sign-up.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel or pause your subscription directly from your account settings.

How are the nutritionists certified?

All our nutritionists are registered dietitians (RD) or certified nutrition specialists (CNS) with at least 3 years of clinical experience.
